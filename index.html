What is music

Music is an art form that organizes sound in a way that is expressive, meaningful, and often enjoyable. It typically consists of elements such as melody, harmony, rhythm, and timbre. Music can be created using instruments, the human voice, or digital tools, and it serves various purposes, including entertainment, communication, emotional expression, and cultural storytelling. Music exists in many styles and genres, from classical to rock, jazz to hip-hop, and electronic to folk. It can evoke emotions, bring people together, and even influence mood and behavior. Essentially, music is a universal language that transcends barriers and connects people worldwide.
